Simple Present

Question Answer
What does Alan do? Alan’s a lorry driver.
How old is Alan? He’s twenty-five years old.
How many days a week does he work? He works five days a week.
What time does he get up? He gets up at six o’clock every day.
What does he eat for breakfast? He eats an enormous breakfast.
What does he drink? He drinks two cups of tea.
What does he do after breakfast? Then he kisses his wife.
What time does he leave for work? He leaves for work at half past six.
Where does he have lunch? He has lunch in a transport café.
What time does he come home? He comes home at five o’clock.
Where does he go in the evening? In the evening he goes to the pub.
What time does he go to bed? He goes to bed at ten o’clock.

JUDITH.
